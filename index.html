Why Syncoes

Syncoes is a simple and affordable education app platform built for schools and coaching institutes. It works on a monthly subscription model, so you can get your own app without heavy setup or long-term commitment. Simply enter your institute code to access your app — no need to upload anything to the Play Store. Syncoes reduces manual work by bringing classes, notes, attendance, and student updates into one easy-to-use app, saving time every day without technical complexity or high costs.

How Syncoes Works

Get your institute app ready in minutes — no technical setup required.

Download the Syncoes App

Students download the Syncoes app from the Play Store — no separate app for each school or coaching institute.

Enter School or Coaching Code

Students enter their school or coaching institute code, and the app instantly switches to their institute’s app.

Access Everything in One App

Students can view classes, notes, attendance, tests, and updates — all from the same Syncoes app.
